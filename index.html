Frequently Asked Questions

Is Abba a replacement for my father of confession?

No. Abba is a spiritual coaching tool, not a substitute for clergy, confession, or professional counseling. It is designed to complement your relationship with the Church.

How does Abba use AI?

Abba uses AI to deliver personalized spiritual guidance grounded in the published teachings of Pope Shenouda III and the Church Fathers. Every conversation is rooted in Orthodox theology.

Is my data private?

Yes. All your conversations and spiritual data are stored locally on your device. There is no cloud sync, no tracking, and no third-party access to your personal information.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your iPhone's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.
