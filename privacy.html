Privacy Policy

Abba is a spiritual coaching app rooted in the Coptic Orthodox tradition. Your privacy is sacred to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data Stored on Your Device

Spiritual profile

During onboarding and coaching sessions, you share information about your spiritual life — prayer habits, areas of growth, and personal reflections. This data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework and never leaves your phone.

Session history

Your past coaching conversations and daily reflections are stored on-device. There is no cloud sync.

Data Sent to Our Server

Coaching conversations

When you have a conversation with Abba, your messages and relevant spiritual context are sent to our backend server to generate a response. Responses are powered by AI (Anthropic Claude). We do not permanently store your conversation content on our servers after the session ends.

No personal identifiers

We do not send your name, email, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information to our servers. Only the spiritual context needed for coaching is transmitted.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not require an account, login, or email address.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not serve advertisements.
  • We do not track your location.

Third-Party Services

Our backend uses Anthropic's Claude AI to generate coaching responses. Messages are transmitted over HTTPS and processed in accordance with Anthropic's data policies. No personally identifiable information is included in these requests.

Data Security

All communication between the app and our server is encrypted using HTTPS. Your on-device data is protected by your device's built-in security, including biometric authentication if you enable it within the app.

Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time by deleting the app from your device. Since we do not maintain user accounts or store personal data on our servers, deletion is immediate and complete.

Children's Privacy

Abba does not collect personal information from any user, including children. The app is suitable for all ages and does not require any personal data to function.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
